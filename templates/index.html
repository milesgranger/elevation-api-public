Get Started #back to top

Simple to use, make a query with a list of tuples representing latitude and longitudes.

Elevations are available at 90m resolution, and any points not found are reported as -9999

Example:

curl "https://elevation-api.io/api/elevation?points=(39.90974,-106.17188),(62.52417,10.02487)"

Results in: 


    {
        "elevations":
            [
                {"lat":39.90974, "lon":-106.17188, "elevation":2606.0},
                {"lat":62.52417, "lon":10.02487,   "elevation":1060.0}
            ]
    }

That's it!
